Our Story

Coffee is a craft, not a commodity.

Ember started as a single roasting drum in a garage. Today we still buy direct from three family farms, cup every new lot blind, and roast only what we'll sell within two weeks.

No syrups, no shortcuts — just well-sourced beans, dialed-in extraction, and a room that feels like someone's front porch.
