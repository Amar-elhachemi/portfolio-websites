Our Philosophy

We serve one menu a night, to nine tables, because that's how many a kitchen can cook properly without rushing. Chef Amine trained in Lyon before coming home to open a room built around restraint rather than spectacle.

Ingredients arrive from three farms we visit ourselves. The wine list is short — twenty-two bottles, chosen to match the menu course by course, not the other way around.
